AS RESTAURANT

Project Title: VA Restaurant Menu Page

Description
A clean, responsive, and visually appealing digital menu for VA Restaurant. This project focuses on a sleek dark-themed UI with vibrant accent borders to showcase a variety of dishes, including South Indian, Chinese, and side specialties.

Key Features
. Modern Dark UI: Uses a deep navy/purple background to make food images and text pop. . Structured Menu Items: Each item is housed in a defined card with high-quality thumbnails, clear descriptions, and pricing. . Visual Highlights: Features a repeating restaurant header image and neon-style pink borders for a modern "dining" aesthetic. . Responsive Layout: Designed to display content clearly across different screen sizes. . Technologies Used.
